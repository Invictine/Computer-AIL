Before booking a hotel you need to decide which room/s you want and down below is some information about the 8 types of rooms we have, after checking them out, you can click the Book Your Room button on top to book your room.

Single Room

100m2 room with single/double beds located from the 15th to the 20th floor

The room has 2 recliners, a sofa, a media consumption area, glass shelves, a cupboard, a kitchen and a bed/2 beds. It also has a bathroom. On top of that you get big balcony overlooking the major attraction at your hotel location.

Single Room+

250m2 room with single/double/quad beds located from the 10th to the 14th floor

The room has 4 recliners, 2 sofas, a media consumption area, glass shelves, 4 cupboards and a bed/2 beds/4 beds. It also has 2 joint bathrooms. Everything else is the same as the Single Room.

Double Room

2 * 'Single room', located from the 5th to the 9th floor

Features are the same as the single room package, just doubled. Two balconies, 2/4 beds, 2 bathrooms etc. It has a door joining both the rooms in the middle.

Double Room+

2* 'Single Room+', located from the 1st to the 4th floor

Features are the same as the Single Room+ package, just doubled. Two balconies, 2/4/8 beds, 4 bathrooms etc. The two rooms are located on two different floors and have a staircase connecting them together.

Studio Suite

400m2 room located from the 21st to the 23rd floor

Has all the features of Single Room+ with a few extra or changed features. The added or changed features are a worktable, work chairs, 5 extenstion ports, beds replaced with Murphy beds and a bigger balcony.

Studio Suite+

400m2 room with an added living room located on the 24th and 25th floor

Has all the features of Studio Suite with a few extra or changed features. The added or changed features are, an added living room 25m2 , an even bigger balcony, studio quality lighting and decorations with a joint recording room.

Presidential Suite

Multiple different rooms, all adding up to a true Presidential feeling, located on the ground and 26th floor

The main rooms include a Studio Suite+ room and 2 Double Room+s connected to a giant living room with a giant balcony.

Cottage*

On selected hotels, there are traditional Shimalayan architectural houses with modern amneties inside.

A Single Room+ room with traditional Shimalayan architecture located either in a massive garden on the hotel roof or the hotel grounds. Only on the Mashobra(garden) and Green Valley(roof) hotels.
